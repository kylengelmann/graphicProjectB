Camera Controls:
Mouse Drag: pivot camera. WASD keys: move camera. Q and E keys: raise and lower camera. Left Shift: hold while moving to increase camera speed.

Ray Trace Controls:
T key: Trace Image. R key: toggle anti-aliasing. Arrow keys: change mirror ball's scale in first scene. F key: toggle stochastic sampling. Z key: toggle directional light. X key: toggle point light. Space key: switch scenes. Number Keys:change maximum number of recursions (0 is 10, turn off stochastic sampling for high numbers, it might crash).
